About Us

Aiswarya Public School Kalakkode, a CBSE provisionally affiliated Senior Secondary School (Affn. No. 930557) is one of the prestigious projects of the ‘Kuncharavila Educational Trust’ founded and established in 2002 by a group of altruistic and dedicated people with a vision to impart ‘Quality Education’ to its students. Recognized by the Kerala General Educational Department vide NOC No:3673/N3/2004/G.Edn. dated 24/07/2004, now we have more than 2000 students from LKG to std XII. Under the able guidance of Sri. R.Ramachandran Pillai, Chairman, Kuncharavila Educational Trust.




Vision & Mission


Vision

Creating Global Citizens with Indian values.

Mission

To impact Quality Education to the students. To uplift the rural inhabitants who are educationally and socially backward. To ensure all round development of the child. To inculcate moral and scientific values to the students.
